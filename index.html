How It Works

Simple. Fast. Reliable.

Crowd intelligence powered by both people and venues — so you always get the full picture.

1

People report what they see

Patrons submit crowd updates directly from the app in seconds. Rate the vibe, note the wait, flag if there's seating. Takes less time than ordering a drink.

2

Venues post official status updates

Partner venues verify and push their own live updates — current wait times, seating status, upcoming closures, and special events. Their data gets a verified badge.

3

You see the full picture, instantly

Venu surfaces the freshest, most reliable crowd data so you can plan your night in seconds — not after a 20-minute Uber ride to find out firsthand.
